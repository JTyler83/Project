Welcome to WhatsUp Girl Gang! My name is Jennifer Corniel. I've created this brand to encompass a feeling inspired by my own toddler, Olivia. Its not just a brand, its a mood!!
Olivia was born with some major heart defects that have required a series of 3 open heart surgeries in just the few years she has been here. Her strength and determination has shined strong right out of the gate!! She was just a few days old and awaiting her first surgery. A nurse came in and said, "This girl has got a temper!" We both laughed and she continued, " she is a fighter and that is what it takes for something like this." I couldn't imagine they could tell her temperment so soon. Than again, they see babies like mine all of the time so she must be right.
She was! Olivia has been bursting with determination and a strong will ever since. Overcoming all that stands in her way with such courage and some major humor to go with it. That is how "WhatsUp Girl Gang" was born. She came in to my room, not even 2 years old to let me know she was dressed like a "WhatsUp Girl". This continued to describe many outfits, songs and entertainment for her. I thought, I need to make some shirts for the "WhatsUp Girl" with Olivia's funny catch phrases to go with it.
I am so excited to share this organic idea inspired by the strongest girl I know with all of you. The first few years of your childs life are something of magic. What they say and how they view this world is hilariously entertaining and so special. I wanted to capture that for all. We are born with such a beautiful confidence and surety of knowing who we are. Cheers to the "WhatsUp Girl" in all of us and may she live on forever!
Our family is growing. Olivia has a beautiful baby sister now, who seems to have her own sweet fierceness! Her name is Vanessa and she is 5 months old. We look forward to our journey and love sharing our products that are inspired with love and hope.
